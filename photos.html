PHOTOS OF WHAT I DO

Photos that I featured are the famous tourist attractions on every town of the province of Marinduque. The following are (from left to right) Boac, Mogpog, Sta. Cruz, Torrijos, Buena Vista and Gasan.

The Immaculate Conception Cathedral Parish

Commonly referred to as Boac Cathedral, is a Roman Catholic church and cathedral in the town center of Boac, Marinduque.

Luzon Datum of 1911

The geographical center of the archipelago and a stone marker marks its significance in Philippine geography. Located at Mogpog, Marinduque.

Maniwaya Island

Located at Sta. Cruz, Marinduque, a long-stretch of powdery and coarse cream sand that the water is blue and clear by the day.

Poctoy White Beach

Located in the coastal town of Torrijos in Marinduque. The beach features a kilometer stretch of fine white sand and pristine blue water that is perfect for swimming and snorkeling.

Malbog Sulfuric Hotspring Resort

The Malbog Hotsprings are volcanic in origin, and hydrogen sulfide is a common gas where sulfur and heat are present. Located at Buena Vista, Marinduque.

Tres Reyes Islands

The islands (Melchor, Gaspar and Baltazar) located at Gasan in Marinduque have soft sand and blue waters where you can spend a couple of hours taking photos, swimming, and beach bumming.
